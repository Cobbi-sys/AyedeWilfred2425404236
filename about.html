Where Excellence Begins

Founded in 2019, Resurrection A/G Academy was born out of a vision to provide a high-standard, transformative education right here in the heart of Ashaiman-Zenu. What started as a commitment to academic discipline has quickly grown into a vibrant center for learning, where we bridge the gap between potential and performance. At our core, we believe that every child in our community deserves access to an education that rivals global standards. We aren't just a school; we are a family. From our modern approach to the curriculum to our focus on character and integrity, we provide a safe and stimulating environment where students feel inspired to ask questions, solve problems, and dream big.
